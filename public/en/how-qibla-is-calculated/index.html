How Qibla Is Calculated

Qible computes great-circle bearing from your position to the Kaaba, then compares it against building axis or user evidence to produce deviation analysis.

Calculation steps

  • Get location coordinates (latitude/longitude).
  • Compute initial great-circle bearing to Kaaba.
  • Estimate building orientation from OSM geometry or manual axis evidence.
  • Report angular difference as deviation.

Tolerance model

Tolerance slider defines acceptable deviation threshold and can be adjusted in the app.
